Working Copy for students

Working Copy is a full featured iOS client for iPhone and iPad. Pro features are available to students for free as part of the GitHub Student Developer Pack.

Clone your repositories onto your mobile device, make edits, commit, push, open pull requests and much more. Repositories are available in other apps on your device.

First step is to install Working Copy from the App Store.

Next you return to this page on your iPhone or iPad and tap here.

Working Copy will open and ask you to authenticate and check your student developer status.

If your Github account is verified as a student you will be able to access all pro features as long as you are logged into this account.
